# SkillMesh Revenue Mesh Design

## Visual thesis

SkillMesh Revenue Mesh should feel like a premium onchain product launch: dark, precise, intelligent, and confident, with the emotional weight of a mission control poster rather than a dashboard.

## Content plan

1. Hero
   - Product name
   - One-line category definition
   - Canonical proof entry
   - Four-step operating loop

2. Support
   - Why this is a product, not a tool
   - Why Scout intelligence matters
   - Why proof matters

3. Product depth
   - Mission launch
   - Live operating surface
   - Organization wallets
   - Scout intelligence
   - Risk gate
   - Evidence and proof

4. Final conversion surface
   - Receipt
   - Funding proof
   - Live purchases
   - Proof ledger

## Interaction thesis

- The first viewport should read like a launch poster with one dominant product promise and one proof CTA.
- The operating surface should feel like a public-facing product shell, not an internal admin layout.
- Motion should stay restrained: drifting background energy, lift on interaction, and clean section reveal.

## Hard style rules

- No card soup in the hero.
- No generic SaaS dashboard framing.
- One accent family led by green, supported by ice blue and warm ember.
- Display typography must carry the product identity.
- Product sections must use short, judge-readable language.
- Proof links must feel first-class, not secondary metadata.

## Product language

- Prefer `onchain operating budget system`
- Prefer `self-funding agent organization`
- Prefer `Scout intelligence`
- Prefer `proof-backed operating record`

- Avoid `generic marketplace`
- Avoid `task dashboard`
- Avoid `internal tool`
- Avoid `agent company`
