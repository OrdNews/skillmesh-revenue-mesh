## Thread Post 2

Core loop:

- `Scout` reads treasury and market context
- `Scout` grades the treasury lane with live signal and token structure data
- `Trader` prepares a live route
- the organization spends its own budget over `x402`
- `Treasury` records proof and mints a receipt on `X Layer`

## Thread Post 3

What makes this version different:

- `Scout` is not just reading price
- it uses `OKX Market`, `OKX Signal`, `OKX Token`, and `OKX Security`
- treasury spend can be held even before execution if the lane stays in review
